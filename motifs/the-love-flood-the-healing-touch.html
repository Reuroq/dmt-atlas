The Love-Flood — the healing touch

motifsPhase 7
How often is this reported?
Loving/embracing interactions in 8.1% of entity encounters (Lawrence 2022, n=139); Davis 2020 found the encounters' emotional palette dominated by joy, trust, and love even though 41% also reported fear.

✦Description

A discrete event, not just an ambient glow: a being embraces, touches, or pours love into the experiencer, often with felt physical force — 'she held me and poured light through me,' 'a wave of love hit me like water.' Lawrence 2022 coded loving/embracing interactions in 8.1% of entity encounters; Michael 2021's 'soother' role covers the same beat. Distinct from the theme 'Overwhelming love & interconnection': the theme is the weather, this is the moment someone turns the hose on you.

✦Interpretations

Experiencers overwhelmingly frame it as healing and many date lasting relief to that instant; researchers can confirm the affect and the aftereffects without adjudicating the toucher.

✦What the sources say

Loving or embracing interactions coded in 139 encounters (8.1%).
— Lawrence, Carhart-Harris, Griffiths & Timmermann — Phenomenology and content of the inhaled N,N-DMT experience (2022)
Predominantly positive emotional responses (joy, trust, love, kindness) despite 41% reporting fear.
— Davis, Clifton, Weaver, Hurwitz, Johnson & Griffiths (Johns Hopkins) — Survey of entity encounter experiences occasioned by inhaled N,N-DMT (2020)
'Soother' and 'guardian' entity functions within the helping/nurturing theme (~53% of sample).
— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)
