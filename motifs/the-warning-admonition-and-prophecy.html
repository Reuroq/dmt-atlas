The Warning — admonition and prophecy

motifsPhase 8
How often is this reported?
Davis 2020: 19% of 2,561 entity-encounter respondents reported receiving a prediction about the future; warning/admonition content per se is unquantified but persistent across corpora from Amaringo's paintings to r/DMT.

✦Description

The tone turns grave: the experiencer is warned — about their habits, their heart, their species, their planet. Ecological admonition is the classic form (McKenna's Logos spoke of the biosphere; Shanon records visions of environmental destruction in the ayahuasca corpus), but personal versions ('stop what you are doing to yourself') are at least as common in community reports. Davis 2020 found 19% of encounter respondents received 'a prediction about the future.'

✦Interpretations

Experiencers frequently act on the warning afterward, which is why it matters clinically; researchers read it as the experiencer's own suppressed appraisals returning with the authority of an external voice. The Atlas takes no position on the sender.
