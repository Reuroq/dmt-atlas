The Gift — an object handed over

motifsPhase 8
How often is this reported?
No survey percentage isolates object-giving; it is a recurrent minority report across McKenna, the Nexus lexicon (which canonized the term 'gifts'), and Erowid vault accounts.

✦Description

A being extends something to the traveler: a jeweled orb, a glyph-covered egg, a toy, a 'sculpture.' The DMT-Nexus Hyperspace Lexicon has a standing 'Gifts' entry — 'sculptures,' 'orbs,' 'Fabergé eggs from mars,' 'glyphs.' McKenna described the elves' offerings as 'celestial toys' urged upon the visitor. The object usually cannot be carried back; the handing-over, not the possession, is the event. Overlaps with but is narrower than the theme 'Being shown a teaching or object' — here the object changes hands.

✦Interpretations

Experiencers often treat the gift as a token of relationship or a seed of later insight; researchers note gift-giving is one of the oldest social scripts and folklore motifs (fairy gifts) available for the mind to stage.

✦Crossings — where this motif meets the older record

The Implant — Quartz, abduction, and the Download — when beings put something inside you
