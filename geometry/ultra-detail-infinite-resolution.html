Ultra-Detail & Infinite Resolution

geometryPhase 2

✦Description

The pervasive sense that the visual field has been 'upgraded to HD' and then far beyond — edges sharper than physically possible, textures resolving into finer and finer detail the closer attention zooms, and an intuition of literally infinite resolution with no smallest pixel. Every element of the pattern seems fully rendered at every scale simultaneously, and travelers report being able to inspect a sub-region and find an entire further world of detail inside it. This 'more real than real' acuity begins at threshold as heightened clarity and becomes, at depth, the felt impossibility of the space — more information than a normal visual field could contain.

What the science says: Klüver noted hallucinatory patterns carrying detail finer than ordinary vision; QRI describes threshold as increased 'level of detail' ('an HD version') and links the infinite-detail feeling to hyperbolic area-growth, where exponentially more surface is packed into each region.

✦What the sources say

Threshold = 'upgrading one's experience to an HD version of it,' with 'the level of detail in one's experience... increased'; hyperbolic geometry packs exponentially growing area/detail into each region.
— Andrés Gómez Emilsson (Qualia Research Institute) — The Hyperbolic Geometry of DMT Experiences (2016)
Documented hallucinatory form constants with fine, replicating, saturated detail exceeding ordinary perceptual resolution.
— Heinrich Klüver — Mescal, and Mechanisms of Hallucinations (1926/1966)
'Hyperreal' — 'unquestionable subjective certainty' that the experience is real, 'probably far more real' than waking life, driven partly by its impossible clarity and detail.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
