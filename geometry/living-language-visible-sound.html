Living language / visible sound

geometryPhase 8

✦Description

Synesthetic geometry in which heard sound becomes seen object — 'things heard become things seen.' McKenna's elves 'spoke in some kind of colored language which condensed into rotating machines'; survey participants saw hieroglyphs and ancient scripts.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Augment: the DMT-Nexus lexicon documents the mechanism — 'audioplasticity,' where 'sounds and words become entangled' across the senses — as the substrate of McKenna's visible Logos. Shanon's ayahuasca typology independently catalogs script/symbol visions, situating visible-language within a cross-tradition pattern.

✦What the sources say

Elves 'making objects come into existence by singing them into existence'; 'use your voice to make an object.'
— Terence McKenna — True Hallucinations / The Archaic Revival / lectures (1991–1993)
Symbolic objects in ~17%: 'Egyptian hieroglyphical writings,' ancient language/hieroglyphics.
— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)
'Audioplasticity' — synesthetic entanglement of sounds and words with the visual field, the felt basis of 'seeing' language.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
Structural typology includes visions of writing, scripts and symbols among figurative visual classes.
— Benny Shanon — The Antipodes of the Mind: charting the phenomenology of the ayahuasca experience (2002)
