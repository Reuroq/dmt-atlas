The Carrier Wave

geometryPhase 1

✦Description

An auditory-vibrational phenomenon at onset — a high-pitched ringing tone, droning frequency, or buzzing 'carrier wave' that resonates 'in every cell of the body,' often paired with a perceptual 'wobble' as the visual field destabilizes. McKenna heard 'a sound like the crumpling of a plastic bread wrapper... and a tone. A hummmm.'

✦Further notes from the corpus

Augment: the onset ring-tone is often experienced as an accelerating, rising vibration ('Acute Acceleration') felt through the whole body, and later couples to structured entity sound — the 'audio windup/windout' as beings arrive and depart. Strassman's volunteers add high-volume entity vocalizations layered over the tone.
