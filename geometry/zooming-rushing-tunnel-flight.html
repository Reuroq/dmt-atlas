Zooming / Rushing Tunnel Flight

geometryPhase 3

✦Description

The 'rush': an overwhelming sensation of acceleration and forward (or upward) motion — being pulled, sucked, launched or elevatored at unimaginable speed through a tunnel, vortex or stack of dimensional layers. Often paired with a rising vibratory tone and a feeling of the body's 'vibratory rate' climbing. Travelers report a 'dimensional elevator' that rises through successive vistas, each opening a chance to exit; or a tornado/typhoon of sensation that sweeps them under. The visual correlate is streaming radial motion — walls of pattern flying past — typically resolving at the far end into the waiting room or breakthrough space.

What the science says: The tunnel-with-forward-flow percept corresponds to Klüver's tunnel/funnel form constant animated by expanding concentric activity in V1; the retinocortical map renders outward-spreading cortical excitation as motion toward or through a central aperture.

✦What the sources say

'Acceleration, Acute' — 'a constantly rising, accelerating vibration felt through the entire body'; 'Dimensional Elevator' — a small rising space 'ascending through a multitude of higher & extra dimensional spaces,' opening periodically on new vistas.

— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)
Volunteers reported 'a startlingly rapid onset' and a sense of consciousness being separated from and rushed away from the body.
— Rick Strassman, M.D. — DMT: The Spirit Molecule (2001)
