Omnidirectional 360° Vision

geometryPhase 6

✦Description

The sense of seeing in all directions at once — a spherical, panoramic awareness with no back-of-the-head blind spot, as though vision has detached from the two forward-facing eyes and become a full surround. Often reported together with a 'view from everywhere / no fixed vantage' quality and with multiple simultaneous perspectives on the same scene. Travelers describe being at the center of a fully enclosing environment, or occupying several viewpoints at once, consistent with the full-immersion 'you are inside it' character of the deeper levels.

What the science says: Aligns with Effect Index/PsychonautWiki's highest geometry tiers ('all-encompassing,' fully immersive perceptual replacement) and QRI's 'full immersion' Waiting Room stage in which partial overlay gives way to total surround.

✦What the sources say

Waiting Room stage: 'full immersion' replaces partial immersion, with 'total and complete synchronization of all senses into a unified synesthetic experience' surrounding the observer.
— Andrés Gómez Emilsson (Qualia Research Institute) — The Hyperbolic Geometry of DMT Experiences (2016)
Full Immersion Visual (FIV) — hallucinations 'of such intensity that one becomes fully immersed within them,' enclosing the field on all sides.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
Geometry Level 7 'all-encompassing' — complete visual replacement creating perception of having 'broken through' into a surrounding reality.
— Josie Kins (and contributors) — Effect Index / Subjective Effect Index (incl. Autonomous Entity, geometry & DMT effect taxonomy) (2011)
