The Grid Plain

Also called: The Infinite Checkerboard, The Blueprint Plane, The Lattice Plain

realms

✦What happens there

Travelers hover, skim, or stand on the plain; watch structures assemble themselves from the gridlines; or receive the strong impression of seeing reality's underlying coordinate system with the content switched off. Some describe data or symbols flowing along the lines. The characteristic response reported is cognitive vertigo — the sense of being shown the format rather than the file. Gallimore's information-theoretic reading of hyperspace is frequently invoked by reporters describing this place.

✦Description

An open landscape rather than a chamber: a flat plane ruled with glowing gridlines running to every horizon beneath a black or faintly luminous sky. Field-study participants described gridwork 'not quite a honeycomb…lattice-work' and one experienced 'raw data outside of me existing…an empty blueprint of the universe.' The place feels pre-architectural — the scaffolding on which worlds would be drawn — and its emptiness is part of the report: vast, silent, coordinate-perfect. Distinct from the enclosed Geometric Palaces & the Lattice: this is the lattice as exterior terrain.
