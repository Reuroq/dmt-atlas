The Domed Cathedral

Also called: the temple, the cosmic opera house, the vault, the infinite cathedral

realms

✦What happens there

The stage for entity contact and the 'performance,' with an atmosphere of sacredness, grandeur, and ceremony — the sense of having arrived somewhere built for a purpose.

✦Description

A vast, often domed or vaulted interior of impossible scale — cathedral, temple, opera house, or palace. McKenna's canonical breakthrough scene: 'tastefully soffited indirect lighting, and the crawling geometric hallucinations along the domed walls.'

✦Further notes from the corpus

The Hyperspace Lexicon records 'The Dahh': the sudden sense of inhabiting 'a sacred cathedral, huge, yet invisible,' with holy reverberation. St John describes the vaulted dome as 'a revered space; the ultimate destination' reached after a fantastic passage — one account gives a solid-red vaulted ceiling over a dull grey floor populated by abstract entities performing magical demonstrations. A Nexus locations report adds 'The Golden Cathedral…complete with the most beautiful music.' McKenna's dome has walls 'crawling with geometric hallucinations…iridescent with deep sheens,' everything 'machine-like and polished and throbbing with energy.'
