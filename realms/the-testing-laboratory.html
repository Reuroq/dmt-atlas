The Testing Laboratory

Also called: The Alien Lab, The Research Facility, The Examination Chamber

realms

✦What happens there

The traveler is scanned, measured, sampled, tested, or observed by beings whose attitude reporters characterize as detached scientific curiosity — interested, unhostile, but not warm. Some reports describe consciousness itself being probed or 'read out'; others describe watching experiments performed on other things or beings. The recurring emotional signature is role-reversal: the psychonaut who came to study hyperspace discovers they are the specimen. Some visitors report the beings seeming surprised at their arrival before resuming work.

✦Description

Distinct in tone from the Operating Theater: where the hospital heals, the laboratory studies. Reporters describe a colder, more utilitarian space — benches, arrays of unidentifiable apparatus, scanning fields of colored light, transparent enclosures, and a pervasive sense of being an object of research rather than a patient. Light is flatter and more forensic; architecture is modular and technological, often described as vast beyond its visible walls. Strassman summarized that his psychonauts 'regularly found themselves hurtled into alien laboratories.'

✦What the sources say

Comparative frame: examination/testing chambers with detached researcher beings mirror abduction phenomenology.
— John E. Mack, M.D. — Abduction: human encounters with aliens (1994)

— Davis, Clifton, Weaver, Hurwitz, Johnson & Griffiths (Johns Hopkins) — Survey of entity encounter experiences occasioned by inhaled N,N-DMT (2020)
Community reports of being 'scanned', 'tested', or 'calibrated' by clinical entities recur across forum threads.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
