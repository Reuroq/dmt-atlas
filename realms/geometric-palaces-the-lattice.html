Geometric Palaces & the Lattice

Also called: the gridwork, the blueprint of the universe, the jeweled palace, crystal architecture

realms

✦What happens there

The visitor perceives an underlying geometric order to existence; structures tessellate, fractalate, rotate and fold; hyper-dimensional objects appear.

✦Description

Realms made entirely of structured geometry — jeweled, crystalline, or lattice/grid architecture experienced as the 'blueprint' or scaffolding of reality ('an empty blueprint of the universe... simple grid... infinite').

✦Further notes from the corpus

Shanon's structural typology finds temples and palaces — 'always marvellous' — among the most common ayahuasca vision contents across disparate cultures. Field-study participants describe honeycombed buildings that evolve and change, lattice-work 'like an amazing beautiful little spiderweb' stretching in every direction, and hyperdimensional structures — rotating multidimensional cubes 'with little cutaways, and each cutaway is its own 3D space.' In the r/DMT corpus, being inside a room was identified in 582 experiences (15.4%), making enclosed architecture one of the most frequent documented settings.
