The Deeper Realms

Also called: the realms of the gods, culturally-themed realms, Dreamtime, the Astral

realms

✦What happens there

Encounters with culture-specific deities and worlds. Notably, the community reports dark entities MORE in these 'other dimensional spaces' than in hyperspace proper — one offered marker of 'genuine' hyperspace is the absence of dark entities.

✦Description

Community-acknowledged realms BEYOND the initial hyperspace entry — often culturally themed (Egyptian, Mayan, Hindu) and framed as 'not Hyperspace, but the realms of those gods'; a gradient, not strictly numbered levels.

✦What the sources say

Culturally-themed realms = 'realms of those gods'; Dreamtime realms; 'the Astral'; a gradient, not discrete levels; dark entities more frequent here.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
Cross-cultural visions of distinct 'higher' realms in the closely related ayahuasca state.
— Benny Shanon — The Antipodes of the Mind: charting the phenomenology of the ayahuasca experience (2002)
