Tunnels & Corridors

Also called: the tunnel, the corridor, the chute, the wormhole

realms

✦What happens there

Translocation — the felt sense of being pulled or launched through a passage at speed, on the way to the waiting room or breakthrough.

✦Description

A transit structure rather than a destination — geometric tunnels or corridors that dominate the field during the rush, conveying fast or accelerating movement toward 'somewhere else.'

✦Further notes from the corpus

The Hyperspace Lexicon adds two vertical/recursive variants: the 'Dimensional Elevator,' a small ascending space that rises through multiple higher-dimensional levels, opening at intervals onto new vistas where the traveler may choose to exit; and 'Folding Rooms,' multidimensional spaces whose architecture 'folds over and in on themselves,' in which entities are said to entangle and enmesh travelers. Both are reported as passage-architectures — corridors that move the traveler rather than being walked.

✦What the sources say

A tunnel-type structure in ~10.3% of reports.
— Lawrence, Carhart-Harris, Griffiths & Timmermann — Phenomenology and content of the inhaled N,N-DMT experience (2022)
'Tunnel-like structures' among canonical NDE themes shared with DMT.
— Timmermann, Roseman, Williams, Carhart-Harris et al. (Imperial College) — DMT Models the Near-Death Experience (2018)
Lexicon entries: Dimensional Elevator (ascending multi-level passage) and Folding Rooms (self-folding entangling architecture).
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
