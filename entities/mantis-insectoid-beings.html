Mantis & insectoid beings

Also called: the praying mantis being, insectoids, mantis surgeons, alien brain surgeons

insectoidPhase 7
How often is this reported?
Occasional but strikingly cross-reported — Strassman's and Luke's canonical 'baffling predictability' type; ~6–11% of the field study's creature categories.

✦Appearance

Towering (often 6–9 ft) insect-proportioned beings — triangular heads, large black eyes, multi-jointed limbs; mantis, moth, or grasshopper-like.

✦Behavior

Clinical and surgeon-like — 'leaning over you doing some weird operation on your brain,' performing examinations or procedures, sometimes 'harvesting emotions.'

✦Communication

Telepathic and businesslike — can 'telepathically reassure everything will be alright' even mid-procedure.

✦Emotional tone

Cool, detached; awe shaded with apprehension — not warm, but often not hostile.

✦Message or purpose

To examine, operate on, or 'fix' the visitor — the strongest single overlap with the abduction 'medical examination' script.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Quantified across three datasets: insectoid proportions in 11% and grey- or mantis-like forms in 6% of encounters (Michael et al. 2021); reptilian-or-insect-like beings in 9.2% of entity reports (Lawrence 2022 Reddit corpus); 'mantises, bees, spiders' appear by name in Strassman's summary of UNM volunteer encounters, several of which involved insectoids conducting procedures — the strongest overlap between the insectoid class and the new Surgeons entry. Meyer's corpus adds hybrid forms: 'scarab creatures with mechanical elements'.

Questions

What are Mantis & insectoid beings in the DMT experience?

Towering (often 6–9 ft) insect-proportioned beings — triangular heads, large black eyes, multi-jointed limbs; mantis, moth, or grasshopper-like. This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are Mantis & insectoid beings reported?

Occasional but strikingly cross-reported — Strassman's and Luke's canonical 'baffling predictability' type; ~6–11% of the field study's creature categories.

When in the experience are Mantis & insectoid beings encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 7 — Entity Contact — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
