Plant spirits & devas

Also called: Plant teachers, Vegetal intelligences, Plant & Object Devas, Mother of the plant

plantPhase 6
How often is this reported?
Effect Index lists 'plants' among documented entity archetypes; the Nexus lexicon names 'Plant & Object Devas' as a class; central to the ayahuasca literature (Narby, Amaringo, Shanon, Harner). In pure vaped-DMT surveys no plant-spirit category is isolated — community-recurrent there, tradition-central in ayahuasca contexts.

✦Appearance

Intelligences that present as botanical: living vines that gesture, flowers with regarding faces, tree-like presences, or beings woven from leaf, root, and tendril patterns. The Nexus lexicon's 'Plant & Object Devas' are 'living, often hyper-intelligent, beings that often resemble plants or objects more than they do what we might normally recognize as sentient beings'. In the ayahuasca literature the plant intelligence often personifies as a maternal or serpentine figure; in vaped-DMT reports it is more often the vegetal architecture itself becoming aware.

✦Behavior

Slow, enveloping engagement: growing around or through the space, blooming displays timed to the experiencer's attention, transmitting botanical imagery (roots, photosynthesis, growth) as experiential lessons. Amaringo's paintings and the mestizo vegetalismo tradition depict plant mothers instructing singers; Narby documents the claim that plant spirits teach knowledge directly.

✦Communication

Imagistic and somatic — cascades of plant imagery, felt growth, and in the ayahuasca tradition, received songs (icaros); rarely conversational in vaped-DMT reports.

✦Emotional tone

Ancient, patient, maternal-to-utterly-indifferent; the Nexus lexicon assigns devas the essential nature 'MYSTERIES & ECSTASY', noting quasi-erotic dimensions ('erogenous zones like pistils and stamen').

✦Message or purpose

Teaching from the vegetal perspective: interconnection, growth, medicine knowledge in the shamanic traditions. Honest caveat: the plant-teacher framing is strongest in ayahuasca/vegetalismo contexts and is partially imported into vaped-DMT culture from there.

✦What the sources say

Lexicon class 'Plant & Object Devas': 'living, often hyper-intelligent, beings that often resemble plants or objects... they seem to have erogenous zones like pistils and stamen' (essential nature: 'MYSTERIES & ECSTASY').
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
The Cosmic Serpent documents Amazonian claims that plant spirits are the source of botanical and medical knowledge transmitted in visions.
— Jeremy Narby — The Cosmic Serpent: DNA and the origins of knowledge (1998)
Amaringo's visionary paintings systematically depict plant mothers and vegetal spirit hierarchies encountered in ayahuasca visions.
— Pablo Amaringo & Luis Eduardo Luna — Ayahuasca Visions: the religious iconography of a Peruvian shaman (1991)
Antipodes of the Mind catalogues plant and vegetal beings as a standing category of ayahuasca visions among his corpus of reports.
— Benny Shanon — The Antipodes of the Mind: charting the phenomenology of the ayahuasca experience (2002)
Harner's ethnography records plant-spirit encounter and instruction as core shamanic technology in Upper Amazon practice.
— Michael Harner — The Way of the Shaman (1980)
'Plants' and 'conscious inanimate objects' are listed among Effect Index's autonomous-entity archetypes.
— Josie Kins (and contributors) — Effect Index / Subjective Effect Index (incl. Autonomous Entity, geometry & DMT effect taxonomy) (2011)
