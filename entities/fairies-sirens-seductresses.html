Fairies, sirens & seductresses

Also called: Flirty Fairies, The seductress, Succubus figures, Winged temptresses

otherPhase 6
How often is this reported?
'Faerie' selected by 8% of 2,561 respondents in Davis 2020 (with 'elf' at 14%); female entities were the single most common tag (24.2%) in the Lawrence 2022 Reddit corpus, within which flirtatious fairy presentations are a recurrent strand; named classes in the Nexus lexicon (Flirty Fairies, Succubus Twins).

✦Appearance

Female-presenting beings whose register is charm: classic winged fairies ('They resemble traditional fairies and often actually have wings... beautiful women who are overflowing with beauty and joy' — Nexus lexicon), dancers, and burlesque-flirtatious figures who 'squirm and pose, bat their eyelashes, wink and play'. Distinct from the Divine Feminine (goddess/mother) by scale and register: these are peers and performers, not enthroned powers. Dark variants exist: the lexicon's 'Succubus Twins' whose glimpsed true nature was crone-like and predatory.

✦Behavior

Flirtation, luring, and delight: beckoning the experiencer deeper, dancing for and with them, showering charm and playful affection. In benign accounts the flirtation is pure exuberant welcome; in cautionary accounts it is bait — distraction from the lesson, or a lure toward entanglement. The community's own taxonomy holds both readings simultaneously.

✦Communication

Body language first — wink, pose, beckon, dance; light teasing telepathy; laughter. Content is invitation rather than information.

✦Emotional tone

Intoxicating, joyous, erotic-adjacent; with a documented uncanny edge in the cautionary accounts. Davis (2020) respondents selected 'faerie' as a descriptor of their most memorable entity in 8% of cases.

✦Message or purpose

Welcome, delight, and invitation — or lure, in the dark reading. Nexus assigns the Flirty Fairies the essential nature 'BEAUTY'.
