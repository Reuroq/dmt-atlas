Orbs & point-light beings

Also called: Light orbs, Spheres of consciousness, Will-o'-wisps of hyperspace, Giant spheres

lightPhase 5
How often is this reported?
Effect Index lists 'giant spheres' among documented autonomous-entity archetypes and orb-form beings recur across Erowid and r/DMT reports; no survey isolates an orb category. Community-recurrent, uncounted.

✦Appearance

Discrete points, balls, or spheres of light experienced as individual conscious beings — from firefly-like sparks that dart and hover to the 'giant spheres' listed among Effect Index's entity archetypes. Often plural, moving with evident intention: circling the experiencer, clustering, dispersing, pulsing in rhythm. Distinguished from generalized light-being/angel forms by their minimal, geometric presentation — awareness with no body at all beyond luminous shape.

✦Behavior

Attentive motion: approaching, orbiting, leading (experiencers describe following an orb deeper into the space), pulsing in apparent response to thought. Groups of orbs coordinate like schools of fish. Some reports describe an orb docking with or entering the experiencer's body or field, accompanied by emotional flooding.

✦Communication

Pulse, color-change, and position — a signal-lamp language — plus direct feeling-transmission at contact. Verbal content is rare; emotional content is strong.

✦Emotional tone

Curious, gentle, often described as innocent or pure; encounters are typically calm and warm rather than overwhelming.

✦Message or purpose

Presence, guidance-by-leading, and emotional transmission (reassurance, love, welcome) rather than propositional teaching.

✦What the sources say

'Giant spheres' and light-form beings are included in the Effect Index catalogue of autonomous-entity manifestation archetypes.
— Josie Kins (and contributors) — Effect Index / Subjective Effect Index (incl. Autonomous Entity, geometry & DMT effect taxonomy) (2011)
Erowid reports recurrently describe conscious points and balls of light that hover, lead, or orbit the experiencer.
— Erowid — Experience Vaults (DMT) (n.d.)
Beings of light and minimal-form presences fall within the study's non-humanoid and 'presence' codings (presences without imagery: 17%).
— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)
Luke's entity-encounter analyses note luminous minimal-form beings as a cross-substance encounter type with folkloric parallels (will-o'-wisps, spirit lights).
— David Luke — Discarnate entities and dimethyltryptamine (DMT): psychopharmacology, phenomenology and ontology (2011)
