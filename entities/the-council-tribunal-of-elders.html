The Council — tribunal of elders

Also called: The Galactic Council, The Elders, The Tribunal, The Committee

teacherPhase 8
How often is this reported?
No survey isolates a council category; it is a recurrent community motif documented in DMT literature — St. John records users reporting 'a galactic council of spiritual elders looking to impart knowledge, laws and instructions for behavior' — and appears regularly in Erowid and r/DMT reports. Community lore rather than a studied category.

✦Appearance

A group — typically described as three to twelve beings — arranged formally: seated in a semicircle, arc, or raised bench, facing the experiencer. Individual members are variously robed elders, light beings, animal-headed figures, or featureless presences; what defines the type is the deliberate, convened arrangement and the experiencer's position as the one appearing before them.

✦Behavior

Deliberation, evaluation, and pronouncement. The council receives the experiencer as if summoned or scheduled, reviews them (sometimes their life, sometimes their conduct in hyperspace), confers among itself, and delivers judgments, instructions, or blessings. Some reports describe being turned away or told 'not yet'; others describe formal welcomes 'as travelers, royalty, or long-lost family'.

✦Communication

Formal and collective: pronouncements experienced as arriving from the group as a whole, telepathic verdicts, or one spokes-being addressing the experiencer on behalf of the rest.

✦Emotional tone

Solemn, weighty, august; ranges from profoundly affirming (being seen and accepted by wise authority) to intimidating (life review before judges). Rarely malicious.

✦Message or purpose

Evaluation and transmission of law-like guidance: how to behave, what to change, what the experiencer is ready (or not ready) for. Frequently entangled with life-review and near-death-experience-style judgment motifs.

✦What the sources say

Documents recurring user reports of a 'galactic council' of spiritual elders imparting knowledge, laws and instructions, with beings welcoming experiencers 'as travelers, royalty, or long-lost family'.
— Graham St John — The Breakthrough Experience: DMT hyperspace and its liminal aesthetics (2018)
Vault reports recurrently describe appearing before assembled groups of elder beings who evaluate, welcome, or instruct the submitter.
— Erowid — Experience Vaults (DMT) (n.d.)
Structural parallel: the NDE life-review before a presence or beings-of-light provides the closest studied analogue to the DMT tribunal scene.
— Raymond Moody — Life After Life (1975)
Community accounts of formal group receptions and hierarchies of beings (e.g., archangels 'ruling' orders of light beings) supply the council's social architecture.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
