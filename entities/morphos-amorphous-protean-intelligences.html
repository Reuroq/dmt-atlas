Morphos — amorphous & protean intelligences

Also called: Blob intelligences, Shapeshifting masses, Nebulous beings, The formless ones

impersonal-intelligencePhase 6
How often is this reported?
Michael et al. (2021) coded non-human 'otherly creatures' in 72% of encounters and featureless silhouettes in 14%, ranges that include amorphous beings; the 'Morphos' class name is DMT-Nexus community taxonomy. Common in community archives, uncounted as a distinct survey category.

✦Appearance

Intelligences without stable form: blobs, clouds, waves, and fields that are unmistakably alive and aware yet refuse fixed shape. The Nexus lexicon's 'Morphos' class 'tend to have fairly unstable forms and can rise and subside out of the Jim Jam like waves... capable of solidifying into phantastic phorms if they so choose', often condensing briefly into mythological shapes 'only far more colorful and multi-dimensional'. Others remain permanently nebulous — described as thinking fog, sentient liquid, or aware plasma.

✦Behavior

Emergence and dissolution: they surface out of the background visual field, engage, and melt back into it, blurring the line between entity and environment. When they solidify a form, it reads as a courtesy — a shape worn briefly so the experiencer has something to address. The Nexus 'Aliens' entry likewise notes 'more amorphous and nebulous intelligences' among the truly alien classes.

✦Communication

Pre-verbal and osmotic: meaning seeps in during proximity rather than being 'said'. When formed, they may briefly adopt the communication style of the shape they wear.

✦Emotional tone

Oceanic and impersonal, with flashes of guidance and wisdom when they condense; the Nexus lexicon assigns Morphos the essential nature 'GUIDANCE & WISDOM'.

✦Message or purpose

Demonstration that form is optional — many experiencers take the shapeshifting itself as the lesson (identity as costume, mind prior to form).

✦What the sources say

Lexicon class 'Morphos': 'fairly unstable forms... rise and subside out of the Jim Jam like waves... capable of solidifying into phantastic phorms', essential nature 'GUIDANCE & WISDOM'; the Aliens entry adds 'more amorphous and nebulous intelligences' among alien classes.
— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)
'Otherly creatures' (non-human, non-animal) coded in 72% of encounters; silhouettes/featureless presences in 14% — the categories into which amorphous intelligences fall.
— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)
Shanon's ayahuasca phenomenology documents beings continuous with their visual surround — entities that condense out of and dissolve back into the visionary field.
— Benny Shanon — The Antipodes of the Mind: charting the phenomenology of the ayahuasca experience (2002)
Erowid reports describe living fog, sentient liquid, and aware shapeless masses as a recurring encounter class.
— Erowid — Experience Vaults (DMT) (n.d.)
