Deceased loved ones & ancestors

Also called: the dead, departed loved ones, ancestors

deceasedPhase 7
How often is this reported?
RARE on DMT (~2%) — and a key DISANALOGY with near-death experiences, where deceased-relative reunions are central. The difference is likely shaped by expectation and context.

✦Appearance

Recognizable deceased people; in the field study humans appeared only as the deceased (no living humans).

✦Behavior

Greet, reassure, or deliver a terse message ('There is nothing here for you, kid!') before the scene shifts.

✦Communication

Telepathic recognition and reassurance; short verbal-feeling messages.

✦Emotional tone

Reunion, comfort, bittersweetness, familiarity.

✦Message or purpose

Reassurance about continuity; guidance or redirection.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Effect Index includes 'ancestors' and 'souls' among documented entity archetypes, and human forms were coded in 17% of encounters in Michael et al. (2021). An important community caution now documented in the new Mimic entry: recurring reports of beings wearing the faces of loved ones whose 'essence underneath' did not match (Nexus 'Strange Visitors'), which colors how the community evaluates deceased-loved-one encounters — treasured, but tested.

Questions

What are Deceased loved ones & ancestors in the DMT experience?

Recognizable deceased people; in the field study humans appeared only as the deceased (no living humans). This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are Deceased loved ones & ancestors reported?

RARE on DMT (~2%) — and a key DISANALOGY with near-death experiences, where deceased-relative reunions are central. The difference is likely shaped by expectation and context.

When in the experience are Deceased loved ones & ancestors encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 7 — Entity Contact — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
