Alien & 'Grey' forms

Also called: the Greys, aliens, extraterrestrials, the visitors

alien-greyPhase 7
How often is this reported?
'Alien' was a top descriptor at ~39% (Davis 2020); the alien phenotype ~16.3% (Lawrence 2022). Strassman noted the resemblance to abduction reports is 'stunning.'

✦Appearance

Classic sci-fi aliens — Greys with large black eyes, or silvery, towering, slender beings whose faces are 'jumbled, abstracted, and leaking colored light,' often in technological settings.

✦Behavior

Investigative and clinical — observe, examine, 'process,' or conduct experiments/probes; can be neutral-curious or unsettling.

✦Communication

Telepathic or 'technologically mediated'; sometimes speech as cartoon-like captions.

✦Emotional tone

Clinical, uncanny; awe at the technological otherness; occasional fear.

✦Message or purpose

To study, examine, or convey that humanity is being observed; sometimes information downloads. The core of the DMT↔abduction overlap.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Quantified: 'alien' was the second most-selected descriptor in the Johns Hopkins survey (39% of 2,561), and alien creatures were tagged in 16.3% of entity reports in the Lawrence 2022 corpus; Michael et al. coded grey- or mantis-like forms in 6% of encounters. The Nexus lexicon adds a phenomenological marker for the truly alien class: 'their minds feel far more foreign than other sorts of entities you encounter... often you come to them in their own realms' — foreignness of mind, not just form, as the class signature.

Questions

What are Alien & 'Grey' forms in the DMT experience?

Classic sci-fi aliens — Greys with large black eyes, or silvery, towering, slender beings whose faces are 'jumbled, abstracted, and leaking colored light,' often in technological settings. This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are Alien & 'Grey' forms reported?

'Alien' was a top descriptor at ~39% (Davis 2020); the alien phenotype ~16.3% (Lawrence 2022). Strassman noted the resemblance to abduction reports is 'stunning.'

When in the experience are Alien & 'Grey' forms encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 7 — Entity Contact — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
