Octopoid & tentacled beings

Also called: Octopus beings, Tentacled intelligences, Cephalopod entities

otherPhase 7
How often is this reported?
Octopoid features were coded in 11% of entity encounters in Michael et al.'s (2021) field study — as common as insectoid features in that dataset. Recurrent in Erowid and r/DMT reports; no other survey isolates the category.

✦Appearance

Beings with cephalopod features: radial tentacled bodies, boneless flowing limbs, iridescent color-shifting skin that displays patterns like a cuttlefish's chromatophores. Scale ranges from hand-sized curiosities to vast tentacled masses filling the visual field. Sometimes hybridized — a humanoid torso with tentacled lower body, or tentacles emerging from geometry itself. In dark encounters, tentacled true-forms are revealed beneath fair disguises.

✦Behavior

Fluid investigation: tentacles reach toward, wrap, taste, or gently palpate the experiencer; color-pattern displays ripple across their skin in what many interpret as language. Generally curious and probing rather than aggressive, though the boneless alien intimacy of contact can overwhelm. The Nexus lexicon's 'Bride of the Devil' account describes a beautiful woman whose true form was 'a hideous hyperdimensional mass of writhing, purplish-black tentacles resembling thorny vines' — the tentacled form as concealed nature.

✦Communication

Skin-display language (moving color patterns offered as visible speech), tactile telepathy through contact, and wordless transmission of alien but coherent intent.

✦Emotional tone

Deeply alien — awe, fascination, and body-horror unease in varying mixture; among the least anthropomorphic and least emotionally legible of entity types.

✦Message or purpose

Rarely delivers propositional messages; the encounter itself — contact with a radically other intelligence — is the content. Some reports describe being 'read' through touch.
