The Divine Feminine

Also called: the Goddess, Mother Goddess, Cosmic Mother, Mother Ayahuasca, the feminine phenotype, She

divine-femininePhase 9
How often is this reported?
The SINGLE MOST COMMON entity phenotype — ~24.2% of entity encounters (Lawrence 2022), echoed by Lyke's ~24% and near-universal in ayahuasca framing. The real 'face' of hyperspace, far more than the elves.

✦Appearance

A huge, intensely bright feminine presence of light — maternal, nurturing, sometimes erotic; in ayahuasca personified as 'Mother Ayahuasca,' the sentient spirit of the vine. Often felt more than seen.

✦Behavior

Enfolds, reassures, heals; 'caresses away all pain and tears,' shows the visitor their wounds and holds them so the pain can dissolve.

✦Communication

Wordless transmission of love, knowing, and acceptance.

✦Emotional tone

Overwhelming love, tenderness, awe, homecoming; sometimes stern correction.

✦Message or purpose

'You are loved / you are home / you already know.' Healing of emotional and maternal wounds.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Quantified: female entities were the single most common entity tag in the Lawrence 2022 Reddit corpus — 24.2% of entity-containing reports. The Nexus lexicon's 'Mother Goddess' entry gives the register: 'A huge, bright, feminine Goddess... Bright, really bright light, caring, loving, caressing away all pain and all tears'. Distinguish the enthroned goddess/mother from the peer-scale fairy/seductress presentations (new entry) and from the Hive Queen (new entry), which fuses the feminine sovereign with the collective swarm.
