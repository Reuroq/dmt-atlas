Embedded faces — faces in the walls

Also called: Wall faces, Faces in the patterns, The watching surfaces, Face-filled geometry

otherPhase 3
How often is this reported?
The Reddit-corpus analysis (Lawrence 2022) identified explicit face/eye imagery in 90 of 3,778 reports (2.4%), with room/wall distortion in 175 reports — likely undercounts since faces are often folded into general visual descriptions. Ubiquitous as a low-dose motif in community archives.

✦Appearance

Faces emerging from, embedded in, or composing surfaces: walls, ceilings, tapestry patterns, and the geometric lattice itself resolving into eyes, noses, and mouths — from a single vast face occupying a wall to surfaces tiled entirely with small watching faces. Occurs at lower doses with eyes open (room surfaces animating) and within deep-state geometry (pattern-space populated by faces). One report in Meyer's corpus describes ceiling stucco becoming 'little eyes, pointed noses, and giggling mouths... poking out of the ceiling, waving and making faces'.

✦Behavior

Watching and reacting: the faces track the experiencer, change expression in response to attention, whisper, giggle, or simply regard. They rarely detach from their surface — the wall itself has become populous. Often experienced as the first sign that the space is inhabited, before any free-standing entity appears.

✦Communication

Expression and gaze; occasionally murmuring or laughter attributed to the faces. Rarely propositional — the communication is 'you are seen'.

✦Emotional tone

Ranges from whimsical (giggling, waving faces) to deeply unsettling (walls of silent watchers); strongly dose- and set-dependent.

✦Message or purpose

Ambient sentience: the discovery that the environment itself is aware. Many experiencers treat embedded faces as the environment's precursor-form of entity contact.

✦What the sources say

Faces or eyes explicitly described in 90 of 3,778 r/DMT reports (2.4%); room and wall distortion in 175 reports — the coding categories covering embedded-face phenomena.
— Lawrence, Carhart-Harris, Griffiths & Timmermann — Phenomenology and content of the inhaled N,N-DMT experience (2022)
Corpus report of ceiling stucco becoming 'little eyes, pointed noses, and giggling mouths', with little people 'poking out of the ceiling, waving and making faces'.
— Peter Meyer — 340 DMT Trip Reports (and 'Concerning the Nature of the DMT Entities') (2005)
Faces surfacing in walls, patterns, and textures is among the most commonly described open-eye phenomena in Erowid DMT reports, typically at sub-breakthrough doses.
— Erowid — Experience Vaults (DMT) (n.d.)
Effect Index documents face-formation within pattern recognition enhancement and transformations, continuous with its entity-emergence taxonomy (Level 1 entities: 'poorly outlined silhouettes barely distinguishable from background').
— Josie Kins (and contributors) — Effect Index / Subjective Effect Index (incl. Autonomous Entity, geometry & DMT effect taxonomy) (2011)
