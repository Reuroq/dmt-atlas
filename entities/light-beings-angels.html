Light beings & angels

Also called: beings of light, angels, archangels

deityPhase 7
How often is this reported?
A recurring benevolent class; the dominant tone of DMT overall is benevolent (>75%, Davis 2020).

✦Appearance

Beings made entirely of light, so radiant that facial features are washed out; winged/angelic guardians.

✦Behavior

Watch over, guard, uplift, initiate; protective and benevolent; in the lexicon 'Archangels' rule a hierarchy of light beings by their depth of access.

✦Communication

Radiance and benign presence; authoritative transmission.

✦Emotional tone

Benevolence, peace, transcendence.

✦Message or purpose

Protection, blessing, spiritual elevation.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Quantified: 'angel' was selected by 16% of Johns Hopkins survey respondents. The Nexus lexicon adds structure: Light Beings are 'entirely made of light. Details like facial expression are often hard to distinguish because of their strong radiance. Always very benign, often connected to the Central Light', with 'Archangels' described as 'a hierarchy of beings that rule the hierarchy of normal Light Beings'; its 'Angels' entry notes they 'don't necessarily look like typical pop culture angels... tend to emanate light and are often keenly watching the humans present... often hang out in the background'. See also the new Orbs and Choir entries for minimal-form and auditory presentations of the light-being family.

Questions

What are Light beings & angels in the DMT experience?

Beings made entirely of light, so radiant that facial features are washed out; winged/angelic guardians. This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are Light beings & angels reported?

A recurring benevolent class; the dominant tone of DMT overall is benevolent (>75%, Davis 2020).

When in the experience are Light beings & angels encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 7 — Entity Contact — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
