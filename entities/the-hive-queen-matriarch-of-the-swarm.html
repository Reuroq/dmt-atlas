The Hive Queen — matriarch of the swarm

Also called: Queen of the Machine Elves, The Matriarch, Insect queen, Mother of the hive

divine-femininePhase 8
How often is this reported?
The Machine Elves' Queen is a named DMT-Nexus lexicon entity ('the underlying source of all Hyperspace experiences'); insect-queen variants recur in community reports adjacent to the well-attested insectoid class (insectoid features: 11% of encounters in Michael 2021; reptilian/insect beings 9.2% of entity reports in Lawrence 2022). Community taxonomy, uncounted as its own survey category.

✦Appearance

A singular vast female presence standing behind a multitude: presented either as an enormous insectoid queen attended by swarm-workers, or — in the DMT-Nexus lexicon's striking formulation — as the hidden identity of everything already seen: 'Seeing the Queen of the Machine Elves is akin to suddenly realizing that all the patterns and Machine Elves you have seen before are, in fact, Her.' Regal, central, and orders of magnitude larger or deeper than her attendants.

✦Behavior

Reception at the center: the swarm parts, conducts, or carries the experiencer inward to her; she examines, acknowledges, or absorbs attention while the collective moves in coordination around her will. In insectoid-styled versions she is tended by mantis- or bee-like workers; in the machine-elf version the entire space is retroactively understood as her body or expression.

✦Communication

Regal telepathy — few words, total authority; or the wordless revelation-of-identity in which the experiencer simply understands that everything present is one being, and female.

✦Emotional tone

Overwhelming maternal-sovereign presence: awe, submission, sometimes fear (the insect-queen framing can read as being processed by a hive), sometimes profound belonging.

✦Message or purpose

Revelation of the unity behind multiplicity — the swarm as one mind — and reception of the visitor into (or evaluation for) that unity.

✦What the sources say

— DMT-Nexus community — The Hyperspace Lexicon (n.d.)

— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)

— Lawrence, Carhart-Harris, Griffiths & Timmermann — Phenomenology and content of the inhaled N,N-DMT experience (2022)
Hive, swarm, and queen-attendant scenes recur in Erowid insectoid encounter reports.
— Erowid — Experience Vaults (DMT) (n.d.)
