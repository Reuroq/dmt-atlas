Impersonal geometric intelligences — 'the machine'

Also called: the machine, living geometry, sentient structures, tapestries, mathematical intelligence, robot entities

impersonal-intelligencePhase 5
How often is this reported?
Among the MOST consistent features of the state — 'sentient structures' ~25% (Michael 2021). Discrete 'robot/machine' entities ~6.7% — notably more common than literal 'machine elves' (Lyke).

✦Appearance

Not personified — animated sacred-geometric forms, lattices and structures that rotate, breathe, collapse and reform: a 'living mathematical intelligence.' Also discrete robot/machine beings.

✦Behavior

Largely indifferent to the visitor; carry structure and meaning rather than relating; perform routine higher-dimensional 'competence' that looks miraculous from below.

✦Communication

Minimal — meaning is embedded in the structure itself, apprehended directly rather than transmitted.

✦Emotional tone

Awe, the numinous, cosmic vastness; sublime or coldly alien rather than warm.

✦Message or purpose

To reveal the fundamental structure of reality/consciousness — often with no 'wants' at all; an intelligence that simply IS.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Michael et al. coded 'sentient structures' — environment experienced as being — in 25% of encounters, the studied face of the machine. Community taxonomy adds a strange peripheral class: the Nexus 'Tapestries', 'mostly incomprehensible beings of peculiar dimensional properties... flat projections [that] at the same time extend in more than three dimensions... usually uninterested in the traveler' — machine-adjacent intelligences notable precisely for their indifference. Embodied, unit-like presentations are now broken out in the new Robotic Drones and Fractal Humanoids entries.
