The Jester / Trickster / Clown

Also called: the jester, harlequin, clown, the joker, circus ringleader, imps

tricksterPhase 7
How often is this reported?
Occasional — jesters ≈ 6.5% of phenotypes (Lawrence 2022); 'mischievous/jestful' demeanor ~14% (Michael 2021). A canonical Strassman category.

✦Appearance

Clowns, jesters and harlequins in medieval-jester garb; sometimes mechanical clowns or top-hatted 'circus ringleaders' with coats, tails and a baton.

✦Behavior

Playful, mischievous, performative — pull the visitor into a shared 'show,' flash information-dense cards/objects, direct attention; can tip from liberating fun into mockery or cruelty.

✦Communication

Telepathy plus visual metaphor — flashing 'hyperspace tarot cards' and objects that carry information.

✦Emotional tone

Mischief, hilarity, occasional unease; humor that can liberate or humble.

✦Message or purpose

To play, to subvert the visitor's seriousness, and to deliver the 'it's all a game — just enjoy' message.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Now quantified in two independent datasets: jester/harlequin-like beings in roughly 8-11% of encounters in Michael et al.'s naturalistic field study (with 'mischievous/jestful' demeanor coded in 14%), and 'jesters' tagged in 6.5% of entity reports in the Lawrence 2022 Reddit corpus. Strassman's famous list of UNM volunteer encounters begins with 'clowns'. The community distinguishes the prank-playing jester proper from the hyper-intelligent card-flashing Harlequin (see new entry) and from the top-hatted Ringmaster — three registers of the performer archetype.

Questions

What are The Jester / Trickster / Clown in the DMT experience?

Clowns, jesters and harlequins in medieval-jester garb; sometimes mechanical clowns or top-hatted 'circus ringleaders' with coats, tails and a baton. This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are The Jester / Trickster / Clown reported?

Occasional — jesters ≈ 6.5% of phenotypes (Lawrence 2022); 'mischievous/jestful' demeanor ~14% (Michael 2021). A canonical Strassman category.

When in the experience are The Jester / Trickster / Clown encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 7 — Entity Contact — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
