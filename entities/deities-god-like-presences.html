Deities & god-like presences

Also called: gods and goddesses, the Godhead, the Source, Supreme Being, the Logos (as God)

deityPhase 9
How often is this reported?
≈ 17% of entity encounters (Lawrence 2022). The encounter frequently rewrites metaphysics — ~80% said it altered their fundamental conception of reality (Davis 2020).

✦Appearance

Vast god-like presences — a supreme being, the Godhead, archetypal cultural deities (Egyptian, Hindu, Mayan), or a formless 'Universal/Gaian Mind' perceived through the visitor's own framework.

✦Behavior

Radiate authority and cosmic knowledge; may take keen interest or remain transcendent and aloof; range from infinitely compassionate to judging.

✦Communication

Telepathic, archetypal, revelatory; direct knowing 'through presence.'

✦Emotional tone

Awe, reverence, smallness before vastness.

✦Message or purpose

Revelation of ultimate reality; disclosure of 'the nature of things'; sometimes moral reckoning.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Quantified: 'divine' beings tagged in 17% of entity reports in the Lawrence 2022 Reddit corpus; 'religious personage' selected by 11% in Davis 2020. The Nexus lexicon's gods entry adds a characteristic detail: encountered god-forms span 'Hindu, Egyptian, Mayan, Celtic, African and plenty of unrecognizable types' and 'tend to be more sci-fi than you would imagine them from only reading sacred books' — technologized versions of traditional iconography. Culture-specific presentations are now broken out in the new Egyptian animal-headed and Aztec/Mayan entries.

Questions

What are Deities & god-like presences in the DMT experience?

Vast god-like presences — a supreme being, the Godhead, archetypal cultural deities (Egyptian, Hindu, Mayan), or a formless 'Universal/Gaian Mind' perceived through the visitor's own framework. This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are Deities & god-like presences reported?

≈ 17% of entity encounters (Lawrence 2022). The encounter frequently rewrites metaphysics — ~80% said it altered their fundamental conception of reality (Davis 2020).

When in the experience are Deities & god-like presences encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 9 — The Peak / Throne / Apex — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
