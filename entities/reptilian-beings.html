Reptilian beings

Also called: reptilians, lizard beings, jewel-encrusted reptilians, saurians

reptilianPhase 7
How often is this reported?
Occasional and canonical (Strassman/Luke), but among the most culturally LOADED labels — likely shaped by conspiracy/UFO 'reptilian' mythology as much as by the raw percept.

✦Appearance

Reptilian/saurian humanoids and serpents, sometimes 'jewel-encrusted'; green scaled skin, snout-like faces, large oval eyes.

✦Behavior

Ambiguous to unsettling — often cold and observing, occasionally framed as malevolent; less interactive than elves or mantises.

✦Communication

Non-verbal, through presence and action; telepathic when engaged.

✦Emotional tone

Neutral-curious to fearsome; a cold, alien quality.

✦Message or purpose

Often opaque — observation, intimidation, or testing more than clear teaching.

✦Further notes from the corpus

Quantified: serpentine forms in 8% of encounters (Michael et al. 2021); reptilian-or-insect beings in 9.2% of entity reports (Lawrence 2022); 'reptiles' named in Strassman's UNM summary list, including the corpus's most disturbing encounter reports. Community taxonomy (Nexus 'Aliens' entry) files 'the reptilians (of various sorts)' among the genuinely alien classes met 'in their own realms'.

What are Reptilian beings in the DMT experience?

Reptilian/saurian humanoids and serpents, sometimes 'jewel-encrusted'; green scaled skin, snout-like faces, large oval eyes. This describes what people report — the Atlas documents the phenomenology, not a metaphysical claim.

How often are Reptilian beings reported?

Occasional and canonical (Strassman/Luke), but among the most culturally LOADED labels — likely shaped by conspiracy/UFO 'reptilian' mythology as much as by the raw percept.

When in the experience are Reptilian beings encountered?

Most reports place them around phase 7 — Entity Contact — of the commonly-reported journey arc.
