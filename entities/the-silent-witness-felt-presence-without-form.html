The Silent Witness — felt presence without form

Also called: The Observer, The Presence, The Watcher, Felt presence

otherPhase 4
How often is this reported?
Among the better-attested phenomena: presences without imagery 17% and omnipresence 14% in Michael et al. (2021); felt-presence is a standing category in psychedelic and anomalous-experience research (Luke), and Strassman's volunteers reported a powerful sense of an aware presence even without form. Well-documented across sources.

✦Appearance

No appearance at all: an unmistakable someone-is-here without image — location-specific (behind, beside, above) or pervading the whole space. Michael et al. (2021) coded 'presences with no imagery' in 17% of encounters and 'omnipresence' in 14%; Effect Index's Level 1 entities are 'vaguely defined presences' below the threshold of visible form. Distinct from every other entry in this taxonomy by being defined negatively — pure detected other.

✦Behavior

Attention: the Witness watches. It does not act, guide, or interfere; its entire behavior is sustained, evident awareness of the experiencer. Sometimes it accompanies (a felt escort through the experience); sometimes it simply is, before, during, and after the visual content. The Nexus lexicon separately records travelers being placed into 'witness consciousness' — 'a camera mode' of pure perception — by entities, the mirror-image phenomenon.

✦Communication

None, and yet total: experiencers report being completely known — read to the bottom — without a single message. The absence of communication from so evident an intelligence is itself the experience's signature.

✦Emotional tone

Ranges with set: profound companionship and safety (being accompanied), neutral observation (being studied), or dread (being watched by something unaccountable). Frequently described as more real than any visual entity.

✦Message or purpose

None stated — presence as such. Interpretations span guardian, researcher, God, the experiencer's own awareness externalized, and the space itself noticing its visitor. The atlas records all as interpretations.

✦What the sources say

'Presences with no imagery' coded in 17% of encounters and 'omnipresence' in 14% — non-visual entity contact as a substantial minority of all encounters.
— Michael, Luke & Robinson — An Encounter With the Other: a thematic & content analysis of DMT experiences from a naturalistic field study (2021)
Level 1 of Effect Index's entity-visibility scale is the 'vaguely defined presence' — entity contact below the threshold of resolved form.
— Josie Kins (and contributors) — Effect Index / Subjective Effect Index (incl. Autonomous Entity, geometry & DMT effect taxonomy) (2011)
UNM volunteers reported the immediate, pre-visual conviction of an intelligent presence aware of them — often the first and most certain element of the encounter.
— Rick Strassman, M.D. — DMT: The Spirit Molecule (2001)
Luke situates DMT felt-presence within the wider anomalous-experience literature (sensed-presence phenomena across states), supporting it as a robust cross-state category.
— David Luke — Discarnate entities and dimethyltryptamine (DMT): psychopharmacology, phenomenology and ontology (2011)
